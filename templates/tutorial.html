Welcome to your very own Assignment Tracker!

Here’s a quick rundown of how things work:

✅ Add assignments from the home page — include the class, due date, and notes.
✅ Manage class links under “Classes” — so clicking a class takes you straight to its Schoology page.
✅ Log in anytime to see your upcoming assignments, automatically sorted by due date.
✅ Flashing lights — We use multiple flashers to alert you of different things. Red flashing cards indicate an assignment that is pass the due date. Yellow Flashing cards can mean two things, an assignment is due on that day (Flashes darker), or the next day. (Flashes a light yellow)
