1. Overview

This website is designed to help students manage their assignments and classes in a secure and organized way. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide and improve the service. This includes:

  • Username and login credentials (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Class names and assignment details you input
  • Basic usage data (like number of assignments completed) for internal analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Display and organize your assignments and due dates
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the service
  • Provide anonymous insights to improve class engagement (if enabled)

4. Data Protection

All sensitive data, such as passwords, is encrypted using industry-standard methods. We never sell, share, or distribute personal data with third parties.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

This site uses browser cookies or local storage to keep you logged in between sessions. No personal data is stored in cookies.

6. Your Rights

You can request to delete your account or all stored data at any time. Simply contact the site administrator or use the “Delete Account” feature in your settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant updates will be announced on the main page or during login.
