Grade Prediction System

Feature UI Backend

A new prediction tool launches on the first day of the next term, giving users a clear way to test hypothetical assignment grades and instantly see how those scores would affect their final class average.

How it works

The prediction system calculates your updated class average based on a score you enter for an upcoming or missing assignment. The tool evaluates all past assignments with grades and applies your course’s weighting rules to give you a realistic estimate.

  • Collects every assignment you’ve already submitted with a grade.
  • Applies weighting rules for each category (tests, quizzes, homework, etc.).
  • Places your hypothetical score into the correct category automatically.
  • Recalculates your overall average instantly.
  • Shows your before and after average so you can compare the impact.

This helps you know exactly what score you need to reach a certain goal, understand how much impact an assignment has on your grade, and plan ahead for the rest of the term.

How to use it

  1. Open the grade tracker for your class.
  2. Find the new “Prediction” panel.
  3. Enter a score for any upcoming assignment.
  4. See your updated average instantly.
  5. Adjust the number to test different outcomes.

Troubleshooting

  • Low data warning: Predictions may be less accurate when a class has only a few graded assignments.
  • Category mismatch: If an assignment category wasn’t set, the tool may prompt you to assign one before predicting.
  • Wrong weighting: If your course has custom weighting, double-check that class settings are correct.
